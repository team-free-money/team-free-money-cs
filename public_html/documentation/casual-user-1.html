Inaya Khan

Bio:

Inaya Khan is a 20 year old student who recently graduated from CNM with an Associate's degree in Computer Science. Her next step in pursuing her educational goals is transferring to UNM to obtain her Bachelor's degree. Inaya's parents do not have the means to help her with her tuition, so she is looking for federal or state scholarships to help her financially.

Technology:

  • Windows 10
  • Google Chrome
  • iPad with IOS
  • iPhone12 with IOS
  • Social Networks

Proficiency: She is very proficient at use.

Attitudes and Needs:

  • Easy Navigation
  • All information available at one place
  • Trusted and verified information
  • Types of scholarships
  • Amount of funding available
  • Criteria to qualify for scholarships
  • Eligibility for scholarships

Frustrations:

  • Couldn't find all related information at one place.
  • Ads and pop-ups.
  • Hard to find out trusted information.
  • Not meeting the criteria to be qualified for the scholarship..
  • Outdated information.
  • Hard to understand criteria and eligibility.
  • Server failure.
  • She can't pay her college fee.

Role:

A student who is searching for scholarships and has visited different scholarship websites before.

Use Case:

Inaya wants to go to college for her Bachelor's and is looking for scholarships. She found SSF.com by searching online.

User Story:

As a New Mexico undergrad student, I want to be able to find all scholarships and required criteria that pertain to me in one place with a no-nonsense layout

Interaction Flow

  • Inaya clicks a link to SSF.com after seeing the results of a browser search --> SSF.com displays the home page.
  • Inaya types "undergraduate students" in the search bar
  • Inaya clicks the "Search" button --> SSF.com displays a list of scholarships containing the keyword: "undergraduate"
  • Inaya clicks a link on one of the listed scholarships --> SSF.com displays scholarship details and a direct link to the scholarship application.
  • Inaya clicks the direct link --> SSF.com remains in the main window while the direct link opens up in a new tab.
  • Inaya clicks the "Home" button --> SSF.com displays the home page.
